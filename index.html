The problem

A press runs on memory, paper and guesswork.

The daily reality on most shop floors — and what it quietly costs.

Paper that doesn't add up

Reams counted by hand; stock and consumption never reconcile, so reorders are late or blind.

Plates that go missing

Thousands of plates across racks with no reliable location — re-making lost plates burns money and time.

Jobs tracked on WhatsApp

Job status lives in chats and people's heads; nobody knows what's running, pending or stuck.

No traceability

When something goes wrong there's no record of who did what, when — disputes can't be settled with data.

The solution

One platform, built only for print.

Presstropic turns the whole shop floor into a connected system. Each module is a "Buddy" — focused, simple, and linked to the others so data flows end to end.

✓ Purpose-built for printing & job-work — not a generic ERP bent to fit
✓ Modular: adopt one Buddy or the whole suite
✓ Every record traceable, every create/edit/delete logged
✓ Financial-year aware IDs that reset cleanly each year
The Buddy suite

Five modules that run the whole floor.

Issue stock to a job, track its plates, run it on the floor, then dispatch — one thread of data.

Paper inventory

PaperBuddy

Inward, stock and consumption for every paper type & size — always know what you have.

  • Inward register by type, size & supplier
  • Live stock, auto-deducted against jobs
  • Packaging slips grouped per pack size
Plate storage

RackBuddy

Every plate mapped to an exact rack location by series rules. Find any plate in seconds.

  • Rack visualization & series masters
  • Search a plate ID → exact rack-slot
  • Usage logs & misplaced-plate checks
Job cards & production

JobBuddy

Job cards, a process chain, and a live floor monitor showing what's running right now.

  • Process route with machine, operator & finish
  • Live floor monitor, auto-refreshing
  • Production analysis by party, process & machine
Raw material store

StoreBuddy

Inward, issue-to-job, reuse returns and scrap tracking for films, inks and consumables.

  • Combination-wise stock per material
  • Issue against a Job ID, reuse returns
  • Scrap logged per financial year
Dispatch & challans

DispatchBuddy

Delivery challans and dispatch records that close the loop from order to delivery.

  • Raise a challan from a completed job
  • FY-prefixed document numbers
  • Searchable dispatch register & reports
Masters & rates

Everything, defined once

Parties, processes, machines and rate lists — down to each process, subtype and finish. Edit a master and choose to apply the change across every existing record.
